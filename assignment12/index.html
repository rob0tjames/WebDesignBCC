Keepin' Busy

I am a multifaceted creator living in Oakland, CA. I've had the pleasure to work in graphic design, graphic illustration, package design, and apparel design. I am a creative problem solver, and I enjoy working with others to see their vision through.

PSA Press

In 2015 I started PSA Press with two of my best friends. Through PSA, I have had the pleasure to colloborate with many local institutions, as well as across the world. Clients include, the Legion of Honor, Another Planet Entertainment, the Charles Schulz Museum, and the Nakamura Keith Haring Collection in Japan, among many others.
