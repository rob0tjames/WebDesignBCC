Laney Homework

The header image is from a font I created for my final project in a Typography class at Laney College.

I named the font Busy Brown, my son's name, mostly because I couldn't think of anything else.

It is a fully usable font, and the Peace Sign and Yin Yang are glyph's within the font that correspond to unicode. In this instance, I saved the font as an image, to embed. I'd be interested in learning how to embed fonts one day!
