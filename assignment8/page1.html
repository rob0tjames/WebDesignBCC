Why Strawberry & Mint?

Well, the truth is that I just went to Adobe Color and messed around with a complimentary color wheel until I found something I liked. It turned out to be a nice pink and a nice green, Strawberry & Mint seemed to be a good description.

Pondering S & M

I don't know if I've ever had any sort of food that was a combination of Strawberry & Mint, if I did, it wasn't mind blowing enough for me to remember.

More on S & M (lol)
