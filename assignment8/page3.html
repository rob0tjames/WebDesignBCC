Alternative Element Organization

Without using relative or absolute positioning, what is another way to organize elements on a page?

Hint:

I can't believe it's not butter!

No, not Margarine, MARGIN!

You can use the margin style to organize elements on a page.

Because we don't know the size of a viewer's browser, we can't use an exact number for the margins.

For this reason, if we wanted to center an element, such as our h1 header, we would style the left and right sides of the header to have a margin of "auto." The top and the bottom do no matter, so we set them to a margin of "0."

This centers the header automatically based on the web browser.
