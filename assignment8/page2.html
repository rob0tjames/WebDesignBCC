Why Strawberry & Mint?

Well, the truth is that I just went to Adobe Color and messed around with a complimentary color wheel until I found something I liked. It turned out to be a nice pink and a nice green, Strawberry & Mint seemed to be a good description.

There is a Strawberry Mint

So it turns out there is a type of mint called the "Strawberry Mint." It's a mint leaf with a distinctive flavor that is reminscent of strawberry. Pictures of strawberry mints often include pictures of real strawberries, though the mint itself does not grow fruit.
